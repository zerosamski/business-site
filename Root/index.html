Welcome to ROLLER, your go-to brand for your board. Based in Amsterdam, ROLLER started when we(Kevin and Sam) decided that instead of getting our favorite brands at different stores, we should start a store that sells the stuff we love. Enter ROLLER. Now you can enjoy the stuff that we enjoy. And don't worry: we test everything before we sell it, so you can be sure we continue to only offer stuff we think is awesome.

Whether you are looking for a electric board to cover some distance, want to go downhill, or prefer the halfpipe: we have it all. We've got boards from Plan B, Boosted Boards, Element, Almost, Alien Workshop, Girl, Enjoi, Baker, and many more brands. Not sure what you're looking for? Browser around! Still not sure? If you visit our store, you can try out the boards before you make a decision.

In need of new wheels? Is your deck at the end of it's life after all that grinding? Is your board hungry for some maintenance? Want to protect your body in case you fall (which, of course, you won't)? Don't worry, we've got you covered! With brands like Bullet, 187 Killer Pads, Sector 9, Thrasher, Deathwish, Obey, Footprint, Skateknife, Volcom, and many others, you are bound to find something you like.
